How you can do your part?

  1. Start in your home! Ensure that you have three separate disposals
    • Garbage/Trash/Waste
    • Glass, Plastic, Metal
    • Paper, Cardboard

  2. Encourage other arounds you to recycle efficiently
    • Share the statistics and information about how plastic and other man made materails are harming our environment. These affects are slowly make their way into our bodies as well. (Read Here)
    • Refer your friends or family to this site to learn more about how to becom sustainable
    • Use re-usable containers as much as possible! Cut our paper plates, plastic silverware, paper-towels,etc.
